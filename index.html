The Experience

More Than
Just a Meal

Every detail — from the Himalayan décor to the scent of spices — takes you on a journey to Nepal.

  • 01

    Authentic Himalayan Atmosphere

    Thangka art, copper lanterns and Nepali artefacts create a truly immersive atmosphere by the Mediterranean coast.

  • 02

    93 Dishes — Something for Everyone

    Meat, fish, vegetarian. Mild to fiery. Our menu covers every taste and dietary preference.

  • 03

    Tourist & Family Friendly

    We welcome tourists, families and groups. English, Spanish and Nepali spoken. High chairs available on request.
